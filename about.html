About the Owner

Canna Bliss District is a well-known seed company that specializes in providing a wide variety of cannabis seeds to growers of all levels. Established with the goal of offering high-quality genetics, the company prides itself on its extensive selection of high THC seeds, including feminized, autoflowering, and regular seeds. Their products cater to both recreational and medicinal cannabis users, aiming to deliver robust plant performance and optimal yields. Homegrown Cannabis Co. boasts a diverse catalog of cannabis ranging from feminized like Strawberry Cough Feminized Cannabis Seeds, regular seeds like Bruce Banner Regular, Autoflower Seeds like White Widow Autoflower Cannabis Seeds, and fast version high THC seeds like Skunk Fast Version. Homegrown Cannabis Co. regularly offers deals and discounts, which are particularly beneficial for bulk buyers and repeat customers. Promotions often include buy-one-get-one-free, seasonal sales, which include timed discounts during major holidays and growing seasons, and loyalty rewards for returning customers.
